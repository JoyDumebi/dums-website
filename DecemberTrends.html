An Autobiography about Myself, Miss Dumebi Ekwubi

Article

Ekwubi Dumebi Joy (born July 13, 199*), better known by her padis as 'Dum-Dum',
is a Nigerian singer, model, fashion designer, frontend engineer, teacher and expert curriculum developer.
She is known for her resilience towards difficult tasks and classy touch she adds to whatever she touches. As a singer,
she also has an impressively high range and sings on five octaves and a basic use of the whistle register.
As of 1st October, 2021, she opened the platform
by singing the Nigerian National Anthem. Her cheerful and
friendly nature have opened doors and opportunities for her.

Born in Ketu, Lagos, Dumebi was raised there too, beginning her career as a fashion designer for her family and friends.
While studying in the Federal University of Benin, UNIBEN, she continued her educational studies in English and literature while taking some training as a fashion designer in local companies and artisan stores near by.
Over time, she began to make designs for students and soon enough, got norminated for the award of the best fashion designer in UNIBEN.
